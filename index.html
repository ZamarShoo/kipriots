Allocation of supplier

Selection of appropriate suppliers in SCMS is a challenging issue because it requires range of evaluation criteria, which are characterized with complexity, elusiveness and uncertainty in nature. Our team developed extensive registrar of manufacturers, expeditors, warehouses, certification laboratories and other related to SCMS stakeholders. We apply technological evaluation framework to assist the expert group to select the optimal supplier in SCMS. The rationales for the evaluation framework are based upon multi-criteria decision-making analysis, that can select the most appropriate alternative from a finite set of alternatives with reference to multiple conflicting criteria.

We can reach all Mainland China throughout diverse agreements and strategic alliances. We work with Laboratories certified by CNAS giving our clients quality service with fast and efficient response.

Our agents will go to any factory in China within 3 to 4 days and deliver a detailed inspection audit to the client.
